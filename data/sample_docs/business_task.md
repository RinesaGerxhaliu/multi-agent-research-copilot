# Business Task Definition

**Task ID:** BT-001  
**Submitted By:** Product Management Team  
**Submission Date:** December 2024  

---

## Business Context
The organization is preparing for the Q2 launch of the **Advanced Analytics Dashboard**, a
key strategic feature aimed at improving data-driven decision-making for enterprise users.
The launch timeline is tightly coupled with ongoing infrastructure and security initiatives.

---

## Business Question
Which actions should the engineering team prioritize during Q2 to ensure the timely,
secure, and high-quality delivery of the Advanced Analytics Dashboard?

---

## Business Objectives
- Deliver the Advanced Analytics Dashboard by **Week 16**
- Minimize risks related to database migration and security approvals
- Ensure the solution meets established performance and security standards
- Maintain alignment with the overall Q2 product roadmap

---

## Constraints and Assumptions
- The database migration to PostgreSQL must be completed before analytics development
- No additional development budget has been approved for Q2
- A completed security review is mandatory prior to production release
- Existing engineering resources must be used

---

## Expected Output
The system should produce a **structured, decision-ready deliverable** that includes:
- A prioritized execution plan
- Identified risks and dependencies
- Clearly assigned owners and timelines
- Evidence-backed recommendations with source citations

---

## Success Criteria
- All recommended actions are traceable to supporting documents
- Any unsupported claims are explicitly flagged as  
  **“Not found in sources”**
- The final output is suitable for direct review by engineering leadership
